For Women: By a Woman


~ SDG 5 aims at schieving gender equality and to empower women and girls to rise to the occasion and reach the rankings that men have achieved, mainly in leadership.


~ In many places globally, women are denied opportunities to work, study, and lead.


~ This is mainly caused by gender biases, cultural norms and barriers in the institution. (CLI, 2025)


~ Women often struggle; not because that they are weak, but because they are judged differently from men.


~ Because of this, we must make a change to this:


  • First, we can initiate negotiations with governments and organisations to enforce strict laws that allow women to have jobs

  • Second, by changing the cultural norms. By educating the people on gender equality and women empowerment, more of the discrminative cultural norms will be elminated
